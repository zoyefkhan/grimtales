Types of Notifications

GrimTales sends notifications for the following events:

📖
New Chapter — A novel you follow has published a new chapter.
💬
Comment Reply — Someone replied to your comment on a chapter.
❤️
New Follower — Someone started following you as an author.
⭐
New Rating — A reader rated one of your novels (authors only).
🔔
System — Important announcements from the GrimTales team.

Viewing Notifications

1
Click the 🔔 bell icon in the navigation bar to go to your Notifications page.
2
Unread notifications are highlighted with a red border. All notifications are marked as read when you visit the page.
3
Click any notification to go directly to the relevant chapter, novel or profile.

Managing Notification Preferences

1
Go to Settings → Notifications.
2
Toggle each notification type on or off using the switches.
3
Email notifications for new chapters and replies can also be turned off here.
Following a novel is required to receive new-chapter notifications. Go to any novel's detail page and click ♡ Follow.

Following Authors

When you follow an author, you'll be notified of their new releases and activity.

1
Visit an author's profile page and click Follow.
2
Authors you follow appear in My Profile → Following.
3
To unfollow, click Following on their profile to toggle it off.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm not receiving email notifications. Why? ▾
Check Settings → Notifications and make sure email alerts are enabled. Also check your spam folder — our emails come from rosewell079@gmail.com.
How do I stop getting chapter alerts for a novel? ▾
Go to the novel's detail page and click ♥ Following to unfollow it. You'll no longer receive notifications for that novel.
Can I turn off all notifications? ▾
Yes — go to Settings → Notifications and toggle off each type individually.
