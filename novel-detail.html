Synopsis

In a kingdom where shadows hold more power than swords, Kael Morn — once the crown's most trusted enforcer — finds himself stripped of rank, title, and purpose after refusing one unjust execution too many.

Exiled to the outer courts, he discovers that the Obsidian Court, the kingdom's most secretive political body, operates not through law but through ancient blood contracts, forbidden magic, and alliances with entities that should not exist.

To survive, Kael must master the very arts he despised: manipulation, deception, and the careful application of darkness. His only allies are a disgraced sorceress with a secret that could shatter the court, a court jester who knows every secret but speaks only in riddles, and a queen who is either his salvation or his ruin.

In the Obsidian Court, loyalty is a weapon, truth is currency, and the wrong word in the wrong ear means death before morning. And somewhere in its shadowed halls, a conspiracy reaches back five centuries — one that Kael is only beginning to unravel.
