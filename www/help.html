Frequently Asked Questions

Is GrimTales free to use? ▾
Yes! GrimTales is completely free for readers. Read unlimited novels, follow authors and leave comments at no cost. Creating an author account and publishing novels is also free.
How do I become an Author? ▾
If you already have a Reader account, go to Settings and click Upgrade to Author — it's free and instant. Or register with the Author option selected.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▾
Click Sign In → Forgot Password → enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
How do I report inappropriate content? ▾
Each chapter and novel has a report button. Click it and fill in the reason. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email us directly.
Can I read novels offline? ▾
Not yet — GrimTales currently requires an internet connection. Offline reading is on our roadmap for a future update.
How do I delete my account? ▾
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data. If you're an author, your published novels will also be removed.
