Reporting Content

If you find a novel, chapter or comment that violates GrimTales guidelines, please report it immediately. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 4 hours.

1
Report a chapter — Open the chapter and click the ⚑ flag icon in the reader controls at the top.
2
Report a novel — On the novel detail page, click Report in the actions section.
3
Report via the form — Use the Report Content page to submit any report with full details.
4
Select a reason (hate speech, graphic violence, copyright violation, spam, etc.) and add any additional details that help our team.
Your report is anonymous by default. The author of the reported content will not know who submitted the report.

Content Guidelines

GrimTales is a platform for dark fiction. While we embrace dark themes, the following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Sexual content involving minors — zero tolerance, reported to authorities
  • Hate speech, racism, discrimination targeting real groups
  • Harassment or threats directed at real individuals
  • Plagiarism or copyright-infringing content
  • Spam, misleading content or fake reviews

Dark themes, morally complex characters, violence, and horror are welcome as long as they serve the story and comply with the content rating you've set.

Privacy Settings

1
Go to Settings → Privacy.
2
Toggle Public Profile to control whether others can view your profile page.
3
Toggle Show Reading History to choose whether your reading activity is visible to others.
4
Toggle Show Library to make your saved novels list public or private.

Your Data Rights

You have full control over your personal data. GrimTales does not sell your data to third parties.

📥
Export your data — Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Export My Data. You'll receive a download link by email.
✏️
Correct your data — Update your profile information at any time in Settings → Profile.
🗑
Delete your data — Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. All data is permanently removed.

For other data requests, email rosewell079@gmail.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can see my reading history? ▾
By default only you. Go to Settings → Privacy and toggle "Show Reading History" if you want it to be public.
What happens after I report someone? ▾
Our moderation team reviews the report and takes action within 4 hours. You won't be notified of the outcome to protect both parties' privacy.
Can I block another user? ▾
User blocking is on the roadmap. For now, you can report any user who is harassing you and our team will take action.
Does GrimTales sell my data? ▾
No. We never sell personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
