Becoming an Author

To publish novels on GrimTales, you need an Author account. It's free and takes seconds.

1
New user: Go to Register and select Author as your account type.
2
Existing Reader: Go to Settings → Profile and click Upgrade to Author — Free.
3
Once upgraded, the Dashboard link appears in your navigation menu.

Creating a Novel

1
Go to your Dashboard and click + New Novel.
2
Enter a Title (required) and a Synopsis — write something compelling that will make readers want to click.
3
Select up to 5 genres that best describe your novel (e.g. Dark Fantasy, Horror, Gothic).
4
Set the Status (Draft, Ongoing, Completed, Hiatus) and Content Rating (All Ages, Teen, Mature).
5
Click Create Novel →. Your novel is now created as a Draft and won't be visible to readers until you publish it.
Cover image: Upload a cover from the novel edit page. Recommended size: 400×600px (2:3 ratio). JPG or PNG, max 2MB.

Writing & Publishing Chapters

1
Go to Dashboard → Write Chapter or click the ✍ icon next to your novel in My Novels.
2
Select the novel this chapter belongs to, then enter a Chapter Title.
3
Write your chapter in the text editor. The word count and estimated reading time update live at the top.
4
Optionally add an Author Note that appears at the end of the chapter for your readers.
5
Click Save Draft to save without publishing, or Publish Chapter › to make it live immediately.
Auto-save: Your chapter is automatically saved every 30 seconds while you type so you never lose your work.

Managing Your Novels

All your novels appear in Dashboard → My Novels. From there you can:

✍
Write — Start or continue writing a chapter for that novel.
👁
View — Preview how your novel looks to readers.
🗑
Delete — Permanently remove a novel and all its chapters. This cannot be undone.

Drafts

Saved but unpublished chapters are stored in Dashboard → Drafts. You can edit them further or publish them when ready. Readers cannot see draft chapters.

Stats & Analytics

Go to Dashboard → Stats & Views to see how your novels are performing — total views, chapter counts, average rating and per-novel breakdown.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should my chapters be? ▾
There's no minimum or maximum. Royal Road-style authors typically write 2,000–5,000 words per chapter. Shorter chapters (1,000–2,000) work well for fast-paced stories.
Can I upload chapters in bulk? ▾
Currently you write chapters directly in the editor. Bulk import (from Word/Google Docs) is on the roadmap.
How do I change my novel's status from Draft to Ongoing? ▾
Go to Dashboard → My Novels → click Edit on your novel → change Status to Ongoing and save.
Will readers be notified when I publish a new chapter? ▾
Yes! Every reader who has followed (bookmarked) your novel receives an in-app notification when you publish a new chapter.
Can I delete a published chapter? ▾
Yes — go to Dashboard → My Novels → the chapter list → delete. Readers who have already read it won't lose their reading history.
