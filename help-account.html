Creating an Account

1
Click Join Free in the top navigation bar or go to Register.
2
Enter your username (3–30 characters, letters/numbers/underscores only), email address and a secure password (8+ characters).
3
Choose your account type: Reader (browse and read) or Author (write and publish). You can upgrade from Reader to Author for free at any time.
4
Agree to the Terms of Service and click Create My Account. You may need to confirm your email address — check your inbox.
Social Sign-in: You can also register instantly with Google or Discord — no password needed.

Signing In

1
Click Sign In in the top navigation bar.
2
Enter your email address and password, then click Sign In to GrimTales.
3
Alternatively, use Google or Discord to sign in instantly with your social account.
Staying signed in: GrimTales keeps you logged in automatically. To sign out manually, click your avatar in the top-right corner → Sign Out.

Resetting Your Password

1
Go to the Sign In page and click Forgot password? below the password field.
2
Enter your email address and submit. A reset link will be sent to your inbox within a few minutes.
3
Click the link in the email, then enter your new password twice to confirm.
Check your spam folder if the email doesn't arrive. The reset link expires after 60 minutes — request a new one if needed.

Editing Your Profile

1
Click your avatar in the top-right corner → Settings, or go directly to Settings.
2
In the Profile tab you can change your username, display name, bio, location and website.
3
To upload a profile photo, click 📷 Upload Photo and select an image (JPG, PNG or WEBP, max 2MB).
4
Click Save Profile to apply your changes.

Upgrading to Author

If you registered as a Reader, you can upgrade to Author at any time — for free, instantly, without creating a new account.

1
Go to Settings → Profile.
2
Click Upgrade to Author — Free in the banner at the top.
3
Your account is instantly upgraded. The Writing Dashboard will now be available from your navigation menu.

Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data — reading history, comments, ratings and (if you are an author) published novels — will be removed.

1
Go to Settings → Danger Zone.
2
Click Delete Account and confirm the action.
Before deleting, consider exporting your data from Settings → Danger Zone → Export My Data. You have 30 days to request your data back after deletion.

Frequently Asked Questions

I can't sign in — what should I do? ▾
Make sure you're using the correct email and password. Try Forgot Password if you've forgotten it. If you signed up with Google or Discord, use the same social sign-in button. See Technical Issues for more help.
Can I change my username? ▾
Yes — go to Settings → Profile → Username field and save. Usernames must be 3–30 characters and contain only letters, numbers and underscores.
Can I have more than one account? ▾
One account per person. If you need both Reader and Author features, upgrade your existing account to Author for free.
I didn't receive my confirmation email. ▾
Check your spam folder first. Wait a few minutes, then try requesting a new confirmation from the login page. Make sure the email address is correct.
