Frequently Asked Questions

Is GrimTales free to use? ▾
Yes! GrimTales is completely free for readers. Browse, read, comment, rate and follow authors with no cost. Creating an Author account and publishing your novels is also free.
How do I become an Author? ▾
Go to Settings and click Upgrade to Author — it's free and instant. Or register a new account and select the Author option. See Writing & Publishing for a full guide.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? ▾
Click Sign In → Forgot Password → enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. See Account Help for more detail.
How do I report inappropriate content? ▾
Each chapter and novel has a ⚑ Report button. Click it, select a reason, add details, and submit. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 4 hours. You can also use the Report Content page.
How do I customise the reading experience? ▾
Open any chapter and click the ⚙ Settings icon in the reader header. You can change font size, font family, background theme (Dark / Sepia / Light / AMOLED) and line spacing. See Reading Help.
Can I read novels offline? ▾
Not yet — GrimTales currently requires an internet connection. Offline reading is planned for a future update. Install GrimTales as a PWA on your phone for the best mobile reading experience.
How do I delete my account? ▾
Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone. If you're an author, your published novels will also be removed.
